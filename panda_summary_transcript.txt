Amidst a desperate need for metal, a conflict unfolds, fueled by theft and raw rice. A group arrives with the intention to liberate a city and bring Lord Shen to justice, discovering imprisoned individuals gathering intelligence. The battle intensifies as someone prepares for a fight, with impending thunder looming. Despite physical constraints, a warrior spirit remains unbroken. Dark revelations about abandonment and parental rejection surface, leading to a moment of surprising survival. A reckless order to fire upon enemies, regardless of friendly fire, highlights the intensity of the situation. Ultimately, the importance of inner peace and letting go of the past is emphasized, urging a character named Shen to define his own future.
